Site Name

Name: Haiti Connect Directory

The name was chosen because it represents the bridge between local organizations and the citizens or donors who need to find them quickly.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a reliable, centralized database of organizations in Haiti. It aims to help users find contact information, physical addresses, and service descriptions for NGOs, local businesses, and community groups to foster better collaboration and accessibility.

Scenarios

The following questions reflect the needs of the target audience:

  • "Where can I find a verified NGO in Pétion-Ville that provides vocational training for young adults?"
  • "I am looking for the official contact number and office hours for the local Chamber of Commerce to register my new small business."

Color Scheme

This plan uses colors inspired by the resilience and heritage of Haiti:

Primary Blue (#17408B): Used for headers, navigation, and primary headings.
Haiti Red (#D21034): Used for call-to-action buttons, accents, and important highlights.
Background White (#FFFFFF): Used for the main content area to ensure high readability.

Typography

Heading Font: Montserrat

Montserrat will be used for all titles (H1, H2, H3) to provide a modern, clean, and professional look.

Body Font: Roboto

Roboto is chosen for paragraphs and lists due to its excellent legibility on both mobile and desktop screens.
