Typography

Heading Font: Montserrat (oubyen font ou chwazi a)

This font will be used for all main titles (H1) and section headings (H2, H3) to create a bold and professional look.

Body Font: Open Sans (oubyen font ou chwazi a)

This font will be used for all paragraphs, lists, and general content to ensure high readability for users.

Color Scheme

The colors are inspired by the Haitian flag and community spirit:

  • Primary: #17408B (Blue) - Used for headers and navigation.
  • Secondary: #D21034 (Red) - Used for buttons and accents.
  • Background: #FFFFFF (White) - Main content background.
