Site Name

Name: Haiti Organizations Directory

Reason: I chose this name because it clearly communicates the website's purpose as a directory where users can find organizations currently operating in Haiti.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a centralized platform where people can access contact information, physical addresses, and service descriptions for various organizations (NGOs, businesses, and associations) serving the community in Haiti.

Scenarios

Here are two scenarios reflecting the needs of the target audience:

  • "Where can I find an organization providing clean water services in the Pétion-Ville area?"
  • "How can I contact an association that helps youth in agriculture to make a donation?"

Color Scheme

These colors are inspired by the Haitian flag and the spirit of the community:

Primary: #17408B (Blue) - Used for the Header, Navigation, and main headings.

Secondary: #D21034 (Red) - Used for buttons, accents, and important highlights.

Background: #FFFFFF (White) - Main background color to ensure text readability.

Typography

Heading Font: Montserrat

This font will be used for all main titles (H1) and section headings (H2, H3) to create a bold and professional aesthetic.

Body Font: Roboto

This font will be used for all paragraphs, lists, and general content to ensure high readability for all users.
