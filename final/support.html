How to Support Haitian Organizations

Support can take many forms: sharing stories, volunteering time, donating, and connecting with local partners.

Donate Responsibly

Choose organizations with clear missions and transparent reporting. Small monthly gifts can fund long-term programs in education, health, and community services.

Volunteer Locally

Volunteer opportunities can include teaching, community outreach, event coordination, and digital support. Reach out directly to organizations to learn how you can help.

Share Their Work

Use social media and personal networks to amplify their stories. Every share helps organizations find new supporters, partners, and donors.

Learn and Connect

Learn about Haiti’s economy, culture, and local needs. Understanding the context makes support more effective and respectful.
